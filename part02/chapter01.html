03 - Learning Evaluation¶

Why¶

We plan, design and deliver learning to do ‘something’ (help newbies get onboard, help new grads understand the business, help new managers understand the ropes, remind everyone about the importance of compliance etc) so of course we (and our clients/decision makers) want to know if we actually did it. That is why we evaluate learning.

What¶

In most cases we do a simple survey/questionnaire of participants after the learning event (e-learning, video, interactive digital content, face-to-face session, all-employee-meeting etc), analyze and make a judgement on the data.

When¶

Just after the learning (as soon as digital learning completes, on last day of face-to-face program)

Where¶

Your survey system (your LMS, Microsoft Forms or some free web service)

Who¶

Your survey system

To Whom¶

The learning participants

How¶

When preparing your learning event/content make sure you decide:

  1. How rigorously you want to evaluate

  2. The points you want to evaluate

  3. The tool you will use to conduct the survey/questionnaire

  4. How you will analyze and present the survey/questionnaire results

Decide how rigorously you want to evaluate¶

Is this a mjor new organizational change event driven the the CEO? In that case a high degree of rigour is demanded. Or is this an annual compliance e-learning that everyone has experienced many times any whose content is mostly the same each year? In which case the default LMS e-learning questionnaire format of 3 or 4 questions would more than suffice.

Decide the points you want to evaluate¶

Of course, the learning aim is a must.

Example¶

Survey questions¶

  1. How satisfied are you with this learning? 1. Extremely satisfied 2. Satisfied 3. Not sure 4. Not satisfied 5. Not at all satisfied

  2. Do you think this learning will help you perform in your role from now on? 1. Yes, extremely 2. Yes 3. Not sure 4. No 5. Not at all

  3. Do you think you achieve the aims of this learning? 1. Yes, definitely 2. Yes 3. Not sure 4. No 5. Not at all

  4. Do you think the learning materials were appropriate to help you achieve the learning aims? 1. Yes, definitely 2. Yes 3. Not sure 4. No 5. Not at all

  5. Do you think the instruction/faciliation was appropriate to help you achieve the learning aims? 1. Yes, definitely 2. Yes 3. Not sure 4. No 5. Not at all
